Here is a fun project you can try to get yourself started! I use a tin can as a container for starting seeds. While I use a sunflower seed in this video (because they're easy to see on video), you can really start any seed using this method.

You will need the following items for this project:

  • Newspaper or similar to protect your work space

  • Soil

  • Seeds

  • A can

  • A nail

  • A hammer

  • Small rocks

  • Popsicle stick or other plant marker
